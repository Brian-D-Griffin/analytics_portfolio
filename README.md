### 1. Active vs. Passive Investment Strategy Optimization
**Course:** ALY 6080 — Integrated Experiential Learning, Northeastern University

A predictive analytics framework designed to assist investment professionals 
in determining whether an actively managed mutual fund will outperform a 
passive benchmark on a risk-adjusted basis. The model leverages multiple

**Domain:** Investment Management, Portfolio Analytics

### 2. Nashville Real Estate Investment Analysis
**Course:** ALY 6020 — Predictive Analytics, Northeastern University

End-to-end machine learning analysis of 22,000+ Nashville property records 
to predict whether properties are undervalued relative to market price. 
Four models were built, compared, and benchmarked — with a final recommendation 
provided to a simulated real estate investment client.

**Models:** Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting
**Tools:** Python (pandas, sklearn, matplotlib), Excel
**Best Model:** Gradient Boosting (76.8% accuracy)
